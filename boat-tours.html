Important Information

What to Bring?

Swimsuit, towel, sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, and camera. We recommend bringing a waterproof bag for your valuables.

Weather Conditions

Tours are weather-dependent. In case of bad weather, we'll reschedule or provide a full refund. Check forecast before booking.

Swimming Ability

Basic swimming skills recommended for tours with swimming stops. Life jackets provided for all passengers regardless of ability.

Age Requirements

Children of all ages welcome. Children under 12 must wear life jackets. Special rates available for children under 6.
