Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a transfer?

You can book by calling us at +382 67 573 413, emailing royalservicesme@gmail.com, or filling out our contact form.

What if my flight is delayed?

We monitor all flights and adjust pickup times automatically. Your driver will be there when you arrive, no extra charge.

Is child seat available?

Yes! We provide child seats upon request. Please let us know when booking so we can prepare.

Can I make stops along the way?

Absolutely! Just inform us in advance and we'll arrange stops at scenic viewpoints or for refreshments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (EUR) and major credit cards. Payment can be made to the driver upon completion of the transfer.

Do you offer round-trip discounts?

Yes! Book your return transfer at the same time and get a special discount. Contact us for details.
